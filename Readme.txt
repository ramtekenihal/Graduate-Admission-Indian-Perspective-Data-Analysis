This is a data Analysis project on Graduate Admission from Indian Perspective. The dataset contains several parameters that are considered 
important during the application for Masters Programs. The parameters included are :

GRE Scores ( out of 340 )
TOEFL Scores ( out of 120 )
University Rating ( out of 5 )
Statement of Purpose and Letter of Recommendation Strength (out of 5)
Undergraduate GPA ( out of 10 )
Research Experience ( either 0 or 1 )
Chance of Admit ( ranging from 0 to 1 ) 


This are some of the questions you can try to answer:-

1. Do GRE & TOEFL scores influence the chance of getting admitted?

2. Does having Research experience makes you score well in GRE & TOEFL?

3. Does the University Rating influence my SOP and LOR Rating?


Note-The decision that someone is admitted or not is subject to your discretion. Please try to make the most appropriate decision and justify your assumptions for the same.
4.What is the average SOP & LOR Rating of students who got admitted? 

5.How does the University Rating improve the chance of getting admitted?

6.What should be your Scores for 0.9 % Chance of Admission?

7.Which among the present parameters has most influence on the chance of admittance?

8.Is there any peculiar relation between research and LOR parameter?

9.Do students with research background have higher CGPA?

10.Is it fair to say students with research background have performed better in everyway? If yes, then Why does Chance of Admit has weakest corelation with Research?
